# Mission Planning Use Cases

## Overview

The Autonomous Mission Planner is designed to demonstrate mission-level autonomy concepts across a variety of unmanned system scenarios.

---

## Use Case 1: Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR)

### Mission

Collect imagery and sensor data from multiple locations while minimizing exposure to known threat zones.

### Inputs

- Mission objectives
- Threat zones
- Platform constraints

### Outputs

- Recommended route
- Risk assessment
- Objective sequence
- Mission feasibility determination

---

## Use Case 2: Border Monitoring

### Mission

Monitor designated geographic areas while remaining within endurance and range limitations.

### Inputs

- Patrol locations
- Platform range limits
- Environmental conditions

### Outputs

- Patrol route
- Coverage sequence
- Risk score

---

## Use Case 3: Infrastructure Inspection

### Mission

Inspect distributed infrastructure assets while minimizing travel distance and mission risk.

### Inputs

- Asset locations
- Platform constraints
- Environmental conditions

### Outputs

- Inspection sequence
- Route distance
- Feasibility assessment

---

## Use Case 4: Disaster Assessment

### Mission

Survey impacted areas following a natural disaster and prioritize locations requiring immediate assessment.

### Inputs

- Assessment objectives
- Environmental risk factors
- Mission priorities

### Outputs

- Prioritized route
- Objective ordering
- Mission summary

---

## Use Case 5: Dynamic Replanning

### Mission

Adapt a mission plan when threat conditions change during execution.

### Inputs

- Existing mission plan
- Updated threat information
- Platform constraints

### Outputs

- Replanning recommendation
- Updated route
- Updated mission risk assessment

---

## Educational Value

These use cases demonstrate how mission-planning systems balance:

- Objectives
- Operational constraints
- Threat exposure
- Environmental uncertainty
- Human decision support
