# Future Capabilities

## Objective

The current implementation provides a foundation for mission-level autonomy experimentation.

Future development will focus on increasing operational realism and decision-support capability.

---

## Threat-Aware Routing

Planned enhancements:

- Alternate route generation
- Threat avoidance
- Route comparison
- Lowest-risk route selection

---

## Environmental Modeling

Potential additions:

- Weather impact analysis
- Terrain constraints
- Visibility effects
- Environmental uncertainty

---

## Multi-Platform Planning

Potential additions:

- Task allocation across platforms
- Cooperative mission planning
- Resource optimization
- Platform coordination

---

## Advanced Risk Assessment

Potential additions:

- Probabilistic risk scoring
- Dynamic threat intelligence
- Mission exposure analysis
- Confidence scoring

---

## Human-Machine Teaming

Potential additions:

- Operator approval workflows
- Explainable decision recommendations
- Decision comparison views
- Mission review dashboards

---

## Mission Replay

Potential additions:

- Mission timeline playback
- Route reconstruction
- Event logging
- Performance analytics

---

## Long-Term Vision

Demonstrate how mission-planning software can combine autonomy, operational constraints, risk assessment, and human decision support within a modular architecture.
